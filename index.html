Burger Recipe

Burger is one of the best fast food loved by many people globally. Today we will discuss about best recipe of burger, you can make at your home easily

Ingredients required

  1. Burger bun
  2. Frozen burger patty
  3. Tomato sauce
  4. Mayonnaise
  5. Fresh onion and tomato
  6. Freshly sliced cheese

Procedure

  • Warm the burger bun on pan.
  • Fry the burger patty.
  • Mix Mayonnaise and tomato sauce in bowl.
  • Apply the mixture on both side of burger bun.
  • place the burger patty on base bun.
  • Put cheese slice on patty
  • Put some onion and tomato.
  • Place the burger in griller and let it be grilled for 30 sec.

Your tasty burger is ready to eat.
